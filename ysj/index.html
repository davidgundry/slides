Intrinsic Elicitation

  1. Necessity

    • Players only engage in data provision that changes game states.
    • Requirement: Embody data provision tasks as the game's interesting mechanics.

  2. Centrality

    • Players select mechanics to maximise utility.
    • Requirement: Make data-providing mechanics as strategically central and effortless as possible.

  3. Veracity

    • Players actuate mechanics to maximise utility.
    • Requirement: Where honest responses are needed, ensure that they have the highest strategic utility and lowest effort, or at least the same utility and effort as any other available option.
